Critical Issues: The Paradox

Root Cause Analysis of Capacity Discrepancies • January 2026

Confidential

The Operational Disconnect

Full Staff

"We are swamped with work."

Empty IDs

"Where are the hours?"

66% Gap

Between Payroll & Revenue

Identified Root Causes

Revision Work Not Logged

Designers treat revisions as "off the clock" courtesies to avoid billing disputes. This massive volume of work is visible to the client but invisible to the billing cycle.

Training Time as "Assigned" Work

New staff shadowing seniors are counted as "fully booked" in the roster, but their hours cannot be billed to Upwork contracts, creating a false sense of capacity utilization.

Administrative Overhead

Internal reporting, Slack communication, and stand-ups consume ~20% of the workday. This "Shadow Work" is necessary but currently untracked and unbilled.

The "Shared ID" Bottleneck

Multiple staff members (e.g., Graphic Design team) funneling work through a single ID (Kainat) creates logistical bottlenecks, forcing work offline to avoid simultaneous login flags.
